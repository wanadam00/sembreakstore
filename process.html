Simple steps to store your items during semester break.

Preparation Tips

Electronics

  • Back up important data
  • Remove batteries if possible
  • Use original packaging if available

Furniture

  • Disassemble large pieces
  • Wrap fragile parts
  • Keep hardware in labeled bags

Bicycles

  • Clean and dry your bike
  • Consider a tune-up before storage
  • Release tire pressure slightly
